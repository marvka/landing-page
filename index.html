Britain is home to 627 wild bird species

The species list is maintained by the British Ornithologists Union (BOU) and is divided into species categories denoting a bird's status on the list.

Britains rare birds

Willow tit

This rare woodlands bird's numbers have declined by 91% since 1970.

Spotted Flycatcher

Impressive insect-catching skills. Numbers now 6 times less than in 1980.

Nightjar

Stealthy bird confined to southern England

Hawfinch

Numbers declined in many areas of Britain and now a rare sight. Has a powerfull bill
