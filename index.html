Britain is home to 627 wild bird species, but numbers are in decline

The species list is maintained by the British Ornithologists Union (BOU) and is divided into species categories denoting a bird's status on the list.

Britains rare birds

Willow tit. 3400 breeding pairs

Spotted flycatcher. Breeding in 36000 territories

Nightjar. 4600 breeding males

Hawfinch. 500-100 breeding pairs
